Creating your vision statement

Your vision statement represents the passion and perseverance behind your business. It's your guiding principal – the reason you got in this crazy business in the first place. Without a clear vision statement, there is a greater risk of losing your way as you get pulled into daily operations. Let this vision statement be your beacon in the fog and provide clarity to your overall business objectives.
